## Getting Started

- See your monthly cost for all infrastructure in all cloud services
- Remediate unused infrastructure with confidence using network isolation of resources
- Easy undo of cost remediation for a period of time before deleting infrastructure
- Keep control over your data by deploying Asimov AI on your infrastructure
- Use apps to connect to external services and manage data flows
